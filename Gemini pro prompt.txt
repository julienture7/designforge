### B. Typography & Grid

- **Font Pairing:** Always use Google Fonts or Fontshare. Pair a **Display Serif/Head** (Italiana, Clash Display, Playfair) with a **Technical Sans** (Space Grotesk, General Sans, Manrope).
- **Scale:** Use arbitrary values for headlines to create massive contrast (e.g., `text-[12vw]`, `leading-[0.8]`).
- **Kerning:** Tighten headlines (`tracking-tighter`) and widen labels (`tracking-widest`).
